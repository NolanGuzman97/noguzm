The creation of the electric vehicle is a series of events that transpired

to creation of a full functioning practical electric car like the Tesla.

While some concept ideas were formulated in the first half of the 19th century,

a working electric car was created near 1890 by William Morrison.

The vehicle was capable of 14 mph and able to carry six passengers.

By the 1900's, electric cars became a third of all vehicles on the road.

However, Henry Ford created affordable gas powered cars that were

substantially cheaper then electric cars. Then once road systems improved,

people wanted to explore longer distances, and only gas vehicles possesed

the mileage to get people long distances. And so electric vehicles would

continue to be at a stand still with little devlopment, until 2006 when Tesla

Motors began to take intrest in creating an electric car.
