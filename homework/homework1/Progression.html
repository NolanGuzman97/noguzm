Tesla Motors has gone well beyond building a functional and luxurious

car. Not only have they set the highest standards, but they influencing more

then just the automotive industry in using electric power. With the creation

of the Gigafactory, Elon Musk is paving the way for an all electrical future.

The Gigafactory does not build the batteries for it's tesla vehicles that get

an impressive 250 mile range and accelerate from 0-60 MPH in as little as 2.4 seconds,

but actually are home to Tesla's new PowerWall and PowerPack. The technologies

are used to store solar energy for houses and provide constant power, even

a power outage occurs. But why is this important? Well it shows that the eagerness

to build powerful lithium batteries to power cars, has caused impressive innovations

in many other areas that use eletrical power. The vision of the Tesla company

is to move away from fossil fuels and to instead be powered by clean electrial energy.
